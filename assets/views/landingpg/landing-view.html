About

Beamer is a lightening fast interface for accessing your website, giving you the ability to add, remove, or update any content at any time no matter where you are.

1. Create Your Beamer

Create your free account with Beamer in seconds

2. Set Your Destination

Embed a simple script into up to five clients to instantly receive live content updates

3. Beam Your Stuff

Use our intuitve interface to design and edit page content to immediately deploy to your site. Select from a variety of themes or easily create your own.

Our Story

From the world's best donut shop, to the worlds best CMS.

  • January 2015

    A Spark of Genius

    Founders Kaityln Walker and Jonathan Dublin describe their vision for a simpler, faster, freer CMS on a Krispy Kreme napkin

  • March 2015

    Funding Secured

    In light of overwhelming positive reception from prospective clients, Beamer secures seed funding from a private investment firm and the dream begins to unfold.

  • March 2015

    Development Begins

    With funding secured, we moved at start up speed to get development underway as soon as possible with the beta version of our API.

  • November 2015

    Public Release!

    (Coming Soon!)

    With help from amazing developers, passionate investors, marketing professionals, interns and volunteers we plan to release our first completely public version this November!
